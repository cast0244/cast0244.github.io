About Me

Hi there, I'm Daniel. I'm currently a student enrolled in Algonquin College's Interactive Media Design program. I've also done Graphic Design as a freelancer for a handful of years now. I'm interested in expanding my abilities, which is why I decided to enter the IMD program. I've done some amateur work in most of the topics that IMD cover, but in order to pick something and be more serious about it I'm now studying all of it to see what I enjoy most.

I help organize local esports events here in Ottawa. I've been part of the organization team for the last 3 years. I've learned a lot about planning events, working with teams and helping patrons. I've also learned a lot about the technical side of things such as streaming events and setting up things like the stage, microphones, cameras and more. Some of my responsabilities include:

  • Set up and tear down
  • Set up and running the stream for the events
  • Making graphics (logos, overlays) or videos (trailers, highlights) for events
  • Commentating when needed
  • Posting announcements and reminders
  • Running the bracket when needed

And some of my hobbies are:

  • Working on graphic design in my free time. To improve my skill, or because someone needs some work.
  • Gamedev. I've only made one simple full game before, but I enjoy the process of putting it together and hope to expand my boundaries soon.
  • Editing Videos

I also compete in these events myself, and have even come 1st a few times. Though as of right now, the events are postponed due to lockdown orders.
